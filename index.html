Our Story

Three Decades of Fashion Excellence

Founded in 1987 by visionary designer Henri Beaumont, our fashion house began with a passion for creating dresses that celebrate elegance and individuality. What started as a small studio has flourished into a renowned brand admired for its artistry and attention to detail.

Today, our third-generation designers carry forward this legacy, blending timeless craftsmanship with modern trends. Each dress is designed to empower women, making them feel confident, graceful, and unforgettable.

Isabelle Beaumont

Creative Director
